FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with the free plan?

Simply click the "Get Started" button and you will be guided through the signup process. No credit card is required for the free plan.

Can I upgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time directly from your account dashboard. The change will be applied immediately.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. We also support payments via PayPal.

Is my data safe with your service?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to ensure your data is always safe and private. Your trust is our top priority.

Do you offer a refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our paid plans. If you are not satisfied, we will provide a full refund, no questions asked.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your plan will remain active until the end of the billing cycle.
